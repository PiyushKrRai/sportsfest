ABOUT US

UEM Jaipur, established in 2012, stands as a top-tier state private university known for its commitment to academic excellence and ovation. Ranked 1st in North India by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India, UEM Jaipur offers cutting-edge programs in fields like Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. With a modern 32-acre campus and a robust placement record, including the highest offer of Rs 72 lakhs per annum, the university is dedicated to shaping future leaders and innovators.

SANGRAM: Unite, Compete, Conquer! This vibrant sports fest is the heartbeat of UEM Jaipur, where students come together for three action-packed days of competition, camaraderie, and celebration. Featuring 24 exciting games, SANGRAM offers something for everyone, from high-intensity sports like football and basketball to strategic challenges like chess.

The event isn’t just about winning; it’s about fostering teamwork, building lasting friendships, and creating unforgettable memories. The energy is contagious as students cheer on their peers, pushing the limits of their abilities and embodying the spirit of sportsmanship. SANGRAM is more than just a fest—it's a festival of passion, unity, and the relentless pursuit of excellence.
